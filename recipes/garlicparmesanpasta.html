Ingredients

  • 225g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup thickened cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fersh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta

    Boil the fettucine pasta in a large pot of salted water until al dente according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside, reserving a cup of pasta water.

  2. Make the Sauce

    In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. Add the Cream

    Pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly. Let the mixture simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.

  4. Add Cheese and Seasoning

    Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. If the sauce becomes too thick, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.

  5. Combine Pasta and Sauce

    Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce. Toss gently to coat the pasta evenly with the creamy garlic Parmesan sauce.

  6. Serve and Garnish

    Divide the pasta among plates. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan cheese if desired.

  7. Enjoy the Creamy Delight

    Serve hot and savor the luxurious, creamy goodness of your homemade Garlic Parmesan Pasta. Pair it with a crisp green salad or garlic bread for a complete and satisfying meal.
